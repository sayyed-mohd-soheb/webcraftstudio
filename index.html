Why Webcraftstudio Exists

Most small businesses and solo founders get priced out of good web development long before they get priced out of a good idea. Agencies quote enterprise rates for a five‑page website, and freelancer marketplaces are a lottery of half‑finished projects and developers who disappear halfway through.

Webcraftstudio was built to close that gap — real engineering discipline, clean code, proper databases, tested deployments, delivered at a price a small business can actually justify, by one person who's personally accountable for everything that ships.
